Lab 6 - Archiving, Compressing, and Unpacking Files

Key Commands:
tar -cvf  → Create archive
tar -xvf  → Extract archive
tar -tvf  → View archive content
gzip / bzip2 → Compress
gunzip / bunzip2 → Decompress

Common Extensions:
.tar   → Archive only
.tar.gz → Gzip-compressed archive
.tar.bz2 → Bzip2-compressed archive

Use Cases:
- Combine multiple files into one for backup or transfer
- Compress large directories to save space
- Verify and extract archives safely
